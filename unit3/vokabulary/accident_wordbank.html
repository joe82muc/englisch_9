Talking about an Accident

Word Bank + Übungen mit 3 Levels und KI-Unterstützung (Render)

📘 Useful Phrases ▼

👮 Police Officer

What is your name, address and phone number?
What is your date of birth?
What did you see?
What time did the accident happen?
Who was involved? How many people were in the accident?
Can you explain that in more detail, please?
Are you sure about that?
Thank you for your help. We will get in touch if we have any more questions.

🧑 Witness

My name is ... my address is ... and my phone number is ...
My date of birth is ...
First ... / Then ... / After that ... / I think that ...
I arrived at ... / I think the accident happened ... minutes before I arrived.
There was a car / van and ... people in the accident.
The car / cyclist / van didn't stop / was going too fast.
Yes, I am. / I think so. / I'm not really sure now.
You're welcome. / No problem.
💡 Tipp: Nutze First ... Then ... After that ... für eine klare, logische witness report Struktur.
