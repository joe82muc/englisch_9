📘 How to form the Past Progressive

▼
was / were + verb-ing  →  I/he/she/it = was  ·  you/we/they = were
Type Example Deutsch
✅ Positive She was walking in the park. Sie war gerade am Gehen.
❌ Negative They weren't sleeping. Sie schliefen nicht gerade.
❓ Question Was he studying? Lernte er gerade?
⚡ while / when While I was cooking, the phone rang. Währ. ich kochte, klingelte das Telefon.

💡 Use it when: something was in progress in the past, and was interrupted by another event (Simple Past).

Level A – Fill in the gaps. Write the correct form of the Past Progressive. Use was/were + verb‑ing.
Exercise 1
Yesterday at 6 p.m., I TV.
📌 Formel: Subjekt + was/were + Verb-ing
Subjekt I → was · Verb watch → watching
✏️ I was watching TV …
Exercise 2
While we the park, it started to rain.
📌 while → laufende Aktion = Past Progressive
Subjekt we → were · visit → visiting
✏️ While we were visiting the park, it started …
Exercise 3
She at her desk when the accident happened.
📌 when + Simple Past unterbricht → laufende Aktion = Past Progressive
Subjekt She → was · work → working
✏️ She was working when the accident happened.
Exercise 4
They football all afternoon.
📌 all afternoon = Dauer → Past Progressive
Subjekt They = Plural → were · play → playing
✏️ They were playing football all afternoon.
Exercise 5
Nick some food while I was working in the garden.
📌 Zwei gleichzeitige Aktionen mit while → beide Past Progressive
Subjekt Nick → was · prepare → preparing (e fällt weg!)
✏️ Nick was preparing … while I was working …
Exercise 6
At 10 a.m. yesterday, the elephants at the lake.
📌 At 10 a.m. = bestimmter Zeitpunkt → Past Progressive
Subjekt the elephants = Plural → were · drink → drinking
✏️ the elephants were drinking at the lake.

✏️ My wallet fell out … while I was walking.

Level C – Write a full sentence. Use the words in brackets. Form the Past Progressive or combine it with the Simple Past.
Exercise 1
Make a negative sentence:
He / study / when the film started. → He was not studying when the film started.
📌 Verneinung: Subjekt + wasn't/weren't + Verb-ing
He → wasn't · study → studying (y bleibt!) · started = Simple Past (bleibt)
✏️ He wasn't studying when the film started.
Exercise 2
Write a question:
What / she / do / at midnight?
📌 Frage: Fragewort + was/were + Subjekt + Verb-ing?
she → was · do → doing
✏️ What was she doing at midnight?
Exercise 3
Combine with while:
I / listen to music. My sister / cook dinner.
📌 Zwei gleichzeitige Aktionen → beide im Past Progressive
I → was listening · My sister → was cooking
✏️ While I was listening to music, my sister was cooking dinner.
Exercise 4
Use when + Simple Past:
We / drive through the park. We / see two lions.
📌 Laufende Aktion (Past Prog.) wird unterbrochen (Simple Past)
drive → were driving · see → unregelmäßig: see – saw – seen
✏️ When we were driving … we saw two lions.
