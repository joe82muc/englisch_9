📘 How to form the Past Progressive

▼
was / were + verb-ing  →  I/he/she/it = was  ·  you/we/they = were
Type Example Deutsch
✅ Positive She was walking in the park. Sie war gerade am Gehen.
❌ Negative They weren't sleeping. Sie schliefen nicht gerade.
❓ Question Was he studying? Lernte er gerade?
⚡ while / when While I was cooking, the phone rang. Währ. ich kochte, klingelte das Telefon.

💡 Use it when: something was in progress in the past, and was interrupted by another event (Simple Past).
