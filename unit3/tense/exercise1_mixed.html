📚 Grammatik-Tipps
Past Simple: Signalwörter: yesterday, last week, ago, in 2010. Regelmäßig → -ed, unregelmäßig → 2. Spalte lernen!
Will Future: Spontane Entschlüsse & Vorhersagen → will + Infinitiv. Signalwörter: tomorrow, I think, probably, next…
Present Progressive: Gerade jetzt → am/is/are + -ing. Signalwörter: right now, at the moment, Look!
Simple Present: Gewohnheiten & Fakten → Grundform (bei he/she/it → -s). Signalwörter: always, usually, every day, often, never.
